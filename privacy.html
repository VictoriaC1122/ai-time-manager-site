Privacy Policy

Your focus data stays simple and local.

AI TimeManager is designed to help users plan tasks, run focus sessions, and review their own history. The app is built with a local-first approach and does not require account creation.

1. Information the app stores

AI TimeManager stores the user's main task, subtasks, session progress, completion history, onboarding state, and notification preference status locally on the device so the app can restore sessions and show past records.

2. Information the app does not collect

AI TimeManager does not require sign-in and does not collect personal profiles, payment information, location data, contact lists, or device-wide browsing activity.

3. Notifications

The app may request notification permission to remind the user when a focus timer ends. Notifications are optional and can be disabled at any time in system settings.

4. Data sharing

The app does not sell user data and does not share local planning data with third parties for advertising purposes.

5. Data retention and deletion

Because the app stores its core planning and history data on the device, users can remove saved progress and history directly inside the app. Removing the app may also remove locally stored data, subject to iOS behavior.

6. Children's privacy

The app is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe sensitive information has been provided through the app, please reach out through the GitHub contact below.

7. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time to reflect app improvements, platform requirements, or legal updates. The latest published version on this site should be treated as the current version.

8. Contact

For privacy questions, please contact the publisher through GitHub: github.com/VictoriaC1122

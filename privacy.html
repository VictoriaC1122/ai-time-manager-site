Privacy Policy

隱私權政策

AI TimeManager 以本機儲存為主要設計方向，專注於協助使用者規劃任務、執行專注流程並查看歷史紀錄。

1. Information the app stores on device

AI TimeManager stores the user's main task, subtasks, session progress, completion history, onboarding state, and notification status locally on the device so the app can restore sessions and show past records.

2. Information the app does not collect from you

AI TimeManager does not require account creation and does not collect personal profiles, payment information, health information, location data, contacts, photos, or device-wide browsing activity.

3. Data transmission and third parties

Based on the current version of the app, task planning data and history are intended to remain on device. The app does not use user accounts, does not include third-party advertising SDKs, and does not transmit focus-session content to a developer-operated server as part of its normal use.

4. Notifications permission

The app may request notification permission to remind the user when a focus timer ends. Notifications are optional and can be disabled at any time in iOS Settings.

5. Data sharing

The app does not sell personal data and does not share local planning content with third parties for advertising purposes.

6. Data retention and deletion

Because the app stores its core planning and history data on the device, users can remove saved progress and history directly inside the app. Removing the app may also remove locally stored data, subject to iOS behavior.

7. Children's privacy

The app is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe sensitive information has been provided through the app, please contact the publisher using the contact method below.

8. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time to reflect app improvements, platform requirements, or legal updates. The latest published version on this site should be treated as the current version.

9. Contact

For privacy questions, please contact the publisher through GitHub: github.com/VictoriaC1122
